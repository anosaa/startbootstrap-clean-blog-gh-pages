Computational Intelligence

for Enhancing Parasitic Organism Detection in Microscopy Images Through Deep Learning

Abstract

Parasitic organisms present a significant global health challenge, particularly in regions with limited medical resources. Early and accurate detection is crucial for saving lives. Deep learning models have revolutionized medical diagnostics by delivering promising results in detecting, diagnosing, and classifying diseases. These models can be further developed to improve accuracy, precision, and recall, enabling not only the detection of diseases but also a deeper understanding of disease dynamics.
