ABOUT US

ABOUT US

We are a group of creative biomedical engineers who are trying to close the gap between healthcare and technology. In our initial study, we successfully classified chronic renal diseases using machine learning techniques. We are currently working on deep learning research to improve the identification of parasitic organisms in microscopy images, overcoming difficult obstacles in the process. We intend to start a hardware-focused project in order to increase our knowledge and acquire hands-on experience in a variety of biomedical engineering aspects.
